Er Kulug-Shad Qaghan

  • Father: Ishbara Tolis Qaghan
  • Role: Tenth Khagan of Western Turkic Khaganate

Life

Er Kulug-Shad Qaghan was khagan of the Western Turkic Khaganate. He was son of Ishbara Tolis Qaghan. He was ruling south of Ili River, which was populated mostly by Nushibi tribes, after his father was murdered. He soon died after he became khagan, leaving the throne to his brother Ishbara Yabgu Qaghan but he was direct killed by some rabels. His son Irbish Qaghan became khagan after that.
